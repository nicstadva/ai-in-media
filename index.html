Our Project

Our project “AI in Media” is dedicated to the development of an AI-based avatar that represents our professor and functions as an interactive e-learning system. This avatar should not only impart knowledge in an innovative way, but also personalize the learning process and support students on their individual educational path. Using state-of-the-art AI technology, speech processing and machine learning, our virtual professor will be able to answer questions, explain content clearly and help students prepare for exams. Our goal is to combine traditional teaching methods with the possibilities of AI to create a future-oriented learning experience that is both interactive and flexible.

Making-Of

Our team met weekly to implement the project step by step. At the beginning, we had to solve licensing problems, but were able to resolve them quickly. A powerful computer was required for the technical implementation in order to be able to use programs such as HeyGen, MetaHuman and Blender efficiently. On this website, we take a closer look at both the implementation process and the technologies used. 

Idea phase

We collected various approaches for our “AI in the media” project, including a website, a 3D model, a cell phone app and our own AI. Finally, we decided to create an AI-based avatar of our professor Dr. Riempp.

Planning phase

In the planning phase, we distributed the task within the group and selected tools to develop the avatar and document the project. We also developed a concept for the design and functionalities of the avatar.

Test phase

Three team members programmed and designed the AI-Avatar, which imitates the appearance and voice of our professor. At the same time, two other team members created an AI-supported website that documented the progress of the project.

Technology

We used state-of-the-art tools to implement our AI-based avatar: HeyGen for speech synthesis, MetaHuman to create a realistic 3D avatar and Blender for customizations and refinements. This combination allowed us to develop an interactive and visually appealing solution.

Website creation with AI

The website was developed using AI tools in particular, whereby we created the design and structure independently in order to create a functional and appealing platform for our project.

HeyGen

HeyGen is an AI-supported speech synthesis tool that enables us to generate realistic and fluent voice interactions for the avatar. It ensures that the avatar can communicate intelligibly and naturally.

MetaHuman

MetaHuman from Epic Games offers a platform for creating highly realistic 3D characters. With precise customization options, we were able to personalize the appearance and facial expressions of our avatar.

E-Learning

Our AI-based avatar not only serves as an interactive companion, but also as a future-oriented e-learning tool. By integrating personalized learning aids and natural language interaction, we create a flexible and engaging learning environment that supports the educational process and responds to students' individual needs.

Module 1: Introduction to text design on screen

Understanding the differences between reading on paper and on screen and the importance of text design for the user experience.

Module 2: Typography on screen - basics and subtleties

Deepen your understanding of fonts, line spacing and visual legibility in digital space.

Module 3: Advanced text design - challenges and solutions

Application of advanced techniques for text design on the web, taking into account technical and aesthetic aspects.
